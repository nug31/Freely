## 🌐 How Freely Works

Freely uses a decentralized network called Nostr to share messages. Unlike regular social apps, Freely does not store your posts on a single company’s server. Instead, it sends messages across many computers. This design helps keep your posts private and in your control.

Freely uses standard cryptography to make sure your messages and identity stay safe from outsiders.

---

## ⚙️ Main Features

- Post messages that others can see but no central company controls  
- Use your own keys based on the Nostr protocol for identity  
- Support for text, images, and links in posts  
- Follow other users through a decentralized system  
- Store posts locally on your device using MongoDB  
- Built with Node.js and Express.js for smooth performance

## 💡 Tips for Using Freely
